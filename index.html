What is the cost per acquisition calculator?
The cost per acquisition calculator is a tool that determines the aggregate cost to acquire one paying customer on a campaign.
How to calculate cost per acquisition?
To calculate cost per acquisition you should use the following formula:
cost per acquisition = total advertisement cost / (total impressions * (click through rate / 100) * (conversion rate / 100))
